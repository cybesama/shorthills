tax
Tax is any charge of money or 
property
 that is imposed by a government upon individuals or 
entities
 that are within the government's authority to collect. This term, however, generally does not include charges imposed in exchange for the provision of specific goods or services (e.g., bridge tolls or sanitation fees).
Although most modern taxes are levied on the basis of economic measurements such as 
income
, consumption, 
property
, and wealth, some governments also impose 
excise taxes
 on the use or sale of specified goods (e.g., gasoline or cigarettes).
